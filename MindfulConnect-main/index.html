When to Seek Emergency Help

Thoughts of Suicide

If you or someone you know is having thoughts of suicide or self-harm, seek help immediately.

Panic Attacks

Severe panic attacks that don't respond to coping strategies may require immediate assistance.

Substance Overdose

If you suspect an overdose of any substance, call emergency services immediately.

Severe Crisis

Any situation where you feel unable to keep yourself or others safe requires immediate help.
